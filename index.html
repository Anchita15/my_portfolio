About Me
Coding Is My Passion

I’m Anchita Ramani, a Master of Computer Science student at North Carolina State University, graduating in May 2026. With a strong foundation in full-stack development and applied machine learning, I bring a research-driven and solution-oriented approach to solving real-world problems. Born in Indianapolis and raised in India, I completed my B.Tech in Computer Science from Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology. I later gained hands-on research experience at the Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, where I worked on deep learning projects in medical imaging and computer vision, including pneumonia detection and driver drowsiness detection systems using CNNs and transfer learning. These efforts led to multiple publications in international conferences and journals. My technical journey began with web development in my first year of undergrad, and over time, expanded into machine learning, data science, and neural networks. At NC State, I’ve continued to deepen my skills through advanced coursework and collaborative projects. I’ve also held leadership roles, including serving as Vice President of my undergraduate tech club, where I organized hackathons and technical workshops. I’m passionate about developing scalable, impactful applications and am actively seeking opportunities in web development, AI/ML, and data-driven innovation.
